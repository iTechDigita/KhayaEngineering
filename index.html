About our company

Khaya Engineering was established in 2002 by the managing director Andile Mtshwane. We specialize in the manufacturing and supply of material handling solutions, such as steel pallets, carriers, and trolleys for the local Port Elizabeth automotive industry, with ISUSU South Africa being our major customer. Khaya Engineering is proudly owned by Andile Mtshwane and Olwethu, Khaya Engineering has Level 1 BBBEE status (135% BEE recognition).

Mission :
Khaya Engineering will provide our customers with professional, innovative, and cost-effective engineering and technical solutions that are responsive to their needs. We will accomplish this through our strong desire to meet our business objectives with a motivated and skilled team

Vision : To be a Sustainable Service Provider of choice in our field.
